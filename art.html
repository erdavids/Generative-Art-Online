The Artwork

I recently fell in love with the concept of generative art. The basic idea is to use programming to make the piece by designing simple rules that are augmented with random aspects. Each result should be statistically unique which is the most amazing part. I've written some tutorials on the subject if you're interested in learning how to create your own.
