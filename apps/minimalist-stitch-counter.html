Minimalist Stitch Counter

When I initially got into crocheting, I learned so much from an incredible community and I wanted to find a way to contribute. I noticed that a lot of people online used stitch counters to track their project progress but I couldn't find a version that matched my expectations of simplicity. I decided to just make it myself.

Download the app here and check the code in this repository.

One of the most important differences with my app is that the user never loses the ability to increment their count. It was incredibly fun to figure out a way to pack all the functionality I wanted into a single page. Users can change between 9 color schemes, save up to 8 projects, and track progress with either individual stitches or rows.

I am actively working on an iOS version.

I hope you enjoy the app. If you have suggestions or feedback, feel free to contact me at thebuffed@gmail.com.
