Pixel Bezier

While not an overly complicated project, this was my first embedded p5.js script. Integrating p5 into my current site was much harder than expected, but I'm satisfied with the results. I'm looking forward to easily incorporating live sketches into other pages.

For this specific process, a pixel is chosen at random and a series of curves are drawn around that spot with its color. As the photo becomes more focused, I will eventually be revealed, standing in my kitchen.
